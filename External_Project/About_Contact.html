About us

Welcome to our shop, where timeless craftsmanship and authentic antique furniture take center stage. We specialize in sourcing and restoring pieces that bring history, character, and beauty into today’s homes.

What began as a small passion for historic furniture has grown into a curated collection of antiques chosen for their quality, originality, and lasting appeal.

Professional restoration that preserves each piece’s integrity, accurate appraisals backed by experience and research, fair purchasing services for individual pieces and estates.

We are dedicated to preserving the charm of the past while helping our customers find meaningful, long-lasting pieces for their spaces. Thank you for visiting, we look forward to helping you discover your next antique treasure.
